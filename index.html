About this project

NovaStore is a design-first storefront demo showing how much polish you can deliver with modern browser APIs. Every interaction—from the hero sliders and quick-view modals to the inventory-aware cart and wishlist—is powered by vanilla JavaScript, live DummyJSON data and LocalStorage persistence. It focuses on delightful UX details such as responsive layouts, dark mode, tactile notifications and keyboard friendly panels, making it a perfect sandbox for experimenting with micro-interactions and e-commerce flows.
